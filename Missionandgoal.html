Mission & Purpose

WHY?

We wanted to do this because we have seen the negative effects when things are placed in the wrong sections. Everyday more and more unnecessary trash is dumped on our planet causing big harm to the people, animals, and environment that inhabit it. But with a little more effort it is easy to sort your garbage correctly and make a big change. We hope that with our program it will be easier for people to make this change.

GOAL

We want to help others so that they change the world for the better. By doing our part, we can help others do their part in saving the Earth!
